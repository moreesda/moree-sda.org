Come and visit us

We are a small and friendly church that welcomes visitors. Often they leave as friends.

Sabbath activities

Sabbath School — 10:00

Sabbath School is a time of Bible study. It is also a time where we get to know each other by sharing and exploring our understanding of the Bible and how to live our lives as Christians.

Worship — 11:30

This is the time for a formal church service, including singing, sharing, and a sermon.

Lunch

We have a pot-luck lunch every week after the worship service. Visitors are most welcome. This is the part of the day when build friendships while we eat together.

Where we are

Corner of Ann & Balo Streets.

Don’t take the bypass — come through town.

Coming south: Cross the Mehi River, pass the roundabout, and turn right at the first corner (just past McDonalds).

Coming north: Turn left at the first street after KFC. If you reach the roundabout, you’ve gone too far.

Coming west: Turn left at the roundabout, then turn right at the first corner (just past McDonalds).

Coming east: Follow the signs to Narrabri, and use the same directions as for coming south.
