Welcome to the blog

Hi everyone, both members and visitors. We’ve created a blog to help the church communicate during the COVID-19 lockdown, since we are unable to meet every week at church. This will help us to send out messages to our church community efficiently, and we can continue to communicate individually via our various methods. We hope you find it useful.

It is possible to subscribe to the blog using a feed reader. That way you will get a notification every time something new is added to the blog.

God’s blessings on each of you.
